About KYS (Bhayander) - Kutch Yuvak Sangh

Together we empower, transform, and build a better future for everyone.

KYS (Bhayander), a subset of Kutch Yuvak Sangh, is an NGO dedicated to empowering communities, transforming lives, and aiming for a better future for everyone. Through its many humanitarian activities, the organization has made significant contributions to the social, educational, and cultural sectors of mankind.

We believe in inclusion and unity: anyone can join us regardless of caste, gender, or religion. We welcome volunteers from all backgrounds, without bias or discrimination on gender, age, caste, or religion.

Our volunteers work with full dedication and selflessness, driven by their responsibility towards society and national welfare. Such commitment places the Kutch Yuvak Sangh among the most respected and elite community organizations.
